Glorious Key Lime Pie

What's the goal?

We are going to make a delicious key lime pie - perfect for breakfast, lunch or dinner!

It's so easy to make -all you need is condensed milk, digestive biscuits, lime, a sprinkle of ginger and some spare lime juice!

Ingredients

  • 300g Digestive biscuits
  • 100 grams of butter, melted
  • 2 large / 3 medium egg yolks
  • Juice and rinds of 3 limes
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 500ml can of condensed milk
  • 300ml double cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 140 degrees
  2. Crush the biscuits
  3. Add ginger and cinnamon to the powdered mix
  4. Grease cake tin with butter
  5. Add crushed biscuits and press down to create a firm base
  6. Place in oven for 5-10 mins. while preparing the filling
  7. In a seperate bowl, add condensed milk, egg yolks and juice of 3 limes and mix well
  8. Gently grate lime peel and add to mix
  9. Add mixture to base and cover to chill overnight
  10. When ready to serve, add spare rinds to top to garnish, slap on some double cream and dig in!
